Pumpkin Pasties

Delicious pastry snacks filled with a sweet pumpkin filling. A Hogwarts Express favorite!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 pack pre-made pie crusts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix pumpkin puree, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg in a bowl.
  3. Roll out the pie crust and cut into small circles.
  4. Spoon a small amount of pumpkin mixture into the center of each circle.
  5. Fold the crust over and crimp the edges with a fork.
  6. Bake on a parchment-lined baking sheet for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
