Chocolate Frog Fudge

Description

A rich and creamy chocolate treat inspired by the famous magical confection!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, melt the chocolate chips and condensed milk over low heat.
  2. Stir in the vanilla extract and salt.
  3. Pour the mixture into a greased or parchment-lined 8x8 pan.
  4. Refrigerate for 2 hours or until firm.
  5. Cut into squares and enjoy!
