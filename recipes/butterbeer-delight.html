Butterbeer Delight

Description

A classic, frothy beverage enjoyed by wizards and witches alike. Perfect for a magical gathering!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cream soda
  • 1/2 cup butterscotch syrup
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a blender, combine the cream soda and butterscotch syrup.
  2. Add the heavy cream and vanilla extract.
  3. Blend until frothy.
  4. Pour into mugs and serve chilled or at room temperature.
